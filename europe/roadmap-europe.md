# Europe - Delivery Roadmap

A 12-to-18-month phased plan anchored to EU AI Act application dates.

## Phase 0 - Mobilise (Month 0–1)
- Sponsor and budget secured.
- Programme charter signed (`../00-foundation/01-program-charter.md`).
- ARC and ARWG stood up.
- Existing AI inventory baseline (best-effort).
- Quick wins: ban prohibited practices today; enable enterprise-grade GenAI tenancy.

## Phase 1 - Comply with already-in-force obligations (Month 1–3)
Already applicable since **2 Feb 2025**:
- Art. 5 prohibitions - confirm none in production or pipeline; block intake.
- Art. 4 AI literacy - roll out M1/M2 training to all staff; record completion.

Deliverables:
- Prohibited-practices declaration signed by ExCo.
- Training completion ≥ 80% within 60 days.

## Phase 2 - GPAI readiness (Month 2–5)
Aligned to **2 Aug 2025** GPAI application:
- Inventory all foundation-model usage.
- Capture vendor documentation, model cards, copyright policies.
- Confirm Code-of-Practice alignment for all material vendors.
- Update contracts (model-change notification, data residency).
- Implement guardrails plane.

Deliverables:
- GPAI register.
- Updated standard contract addendum.
- Guardrail coverage report.

## Phase 3 - Build the foundation (Month 2–6)
- Full inventory (KRI-01 ≥ 98%).
- Policy stack approved.
- Risk taxonomy, methodology and control catalogue adopted.
- AIIA template piloted on 3 representative use cases (one per BU).
- Independent validation function operating.
- Tooling decisions: governance plane, MLOps, observability, guardrails.

Deliverables:
- Inventory.
- Approved policies.
- Three completed AIIAs.
- Tooling decision memos.

## Phase 4 - High-risk readiness (Month 4–12)
Aligned to **2 Aug 2026** High-risk obligations:
- Identify candidate High-risk systems early.
- Build Annex IV technical documentation for each.
- Establish post-market monitoring pipelines.
- Wire up serious-incident reporting workflow with regulator contact list.
- QMS aligned to ISO/IEC 42001 (consider certification on a 2-year horizon).
- FRIA workflow for public-body and applicable private deployers.

Deliverables:
- Annex IV packs.
- Post-market monitoring live.
- ISO/IEC 42001 readiness assessment.

## Phase 5 - Cross-regulation harmonisation (Month 6–14)
- DORA register (financial entities) integrated with AI inventory.
- NIS2 incident-reporting pathway integrated with AI incidents.
- MDR conformity sequencing (medical-device AI).
- Product Liability Directive impact on contracts and insurance reviewed.

## Phase 6 - Embed and assure (Month 9–18)
- Internal Audit AI thematic review.
- External assurance over governance (ISO/IEC 42001 stage 1 + 2 if pursuing certification).
- Board reporting cadence stable; KRIs in green for two consecutive quarters.
- Continuous-improvement backlog established.

## Phase 7 - Continuous (ongoing)
- Track regulatory horizon (delegated acts, harmonised standards, Commission guidelines).
- Refresh tier classifications on regulatory change.
- Annual reassessment of all High-risk systems.
- Annual policy refresh.
