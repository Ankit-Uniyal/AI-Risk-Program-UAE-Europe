## 1. One-page summary

- Portfolio: 14 AI use cases inventoried (up from 9 last quarter). 2 are Tier 2 (high). 0 are Tier 3 (unacceptable). 5 are Tier 1 (limited). 7 are Tier 0 (minimal).
- Two use cases entered formal assessment this quarter: CV-Screening AI (AI-2026-0001) and Customer Service Chatbot (AI-2026-0002). CV-Screening AI approved with conditions; Chatbot approved.
- 11 open risks across the portfolio; 9 with treatment in progress; 2 overdue.
- No incidents, no regulator enquiries, no material breaches this quarter.
- One policy update: AI Acceptable Use Policy v1.2 published; mandatory training assigned to 1,180 staff (completion 84%).

## 3. Spotlight — CV-Screening AI (AI-2026-0001)

| Item | Status |
|---|---|
| Tier | 2 — High |
| Regions | UAE + EU |
| Regulatory exposure | EU AI Act Annex III (employment); GDPR Art. 22; UAE Charter for AI; UAE PDPL (cross-border) |
| Inherent risk score | 25 / 40 |
| Residual risk (target) | 12 / 40 |
| Approval | ARC approved with conditions on 25 Apr 2026 |
| Go-live | 1 Sep 2026 (on track) |
| Open treatments | 4 of 6 (bias test, vendor addendum, transparency package, training) |
